This Combined Utilization of Cutting Edge
Technologies and Leading Software Programs
Allows us to Document, Research, and Investigate
with Extremely Accurate Digital Data

Thomas Elmore with The GeoNAV Group will be presenting his 3D LiDAR scanning and mapping on 2 one-acre parcels of land at Manitou Hassannash Preserve in Rhode Island. 
Eva Gibavic with Ceremonial Landscapes Research will present Tom’s LiDAR merged with her company’s research at the Manitou Hassannash Preserve with a demonstration using ArcGIS’s 3D capabilities. 
NEARA Pennsylvania Coordinator and Moderator David Gutkowski will present his research at a site in Pennsylvania using Stellarium software to map and document solar alignments relating to a series of uniquely placed boulders.
