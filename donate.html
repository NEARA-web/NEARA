Making a Donation to NEARA

100% of what you donate will go to NEARA, via the PayPal Giving Fund, which has waived the usual PayPal fees.

You will be going to the PayPal Giving Fund page for NEARA: NEARA's PayPal Giving Fund.

Enter the amount in the "Other" field above the "Donate Now" button.

Please provide your name and email address so that we can identify the donation.

Click the "Donate Now" button and proceed with the transaction.

Now go to the Giving Fund page:
NEARA's PayPal Giving Fund

Thank you for helping NEARA!
