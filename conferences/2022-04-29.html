Spring 2022 Conference

We finally got to meet in person again, at Camp Wightman in North Stonington Connecticut.

The theme for the conference was: What Science, Pseudoscience, and Scientism have to do with Indigenous Stone Structures.

View the program at Spring 2022 Program.

We did record many of the presentations. Some of the videos will eventually be posted on our YouTube channel. Other videos will only be accessible after logging in on sitedb.org.

Annual General Meeting

We held the annual NEARA general meeting on Saturday morning. Many of the chapter coordinators gave brief reports about past activities and future field trips.
